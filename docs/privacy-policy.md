# Privacy Policy – League Keeper

**Last updated:** February 2025

**App name:** League Keeper (Budget League Tracker)

---

## Overview

League Keeper is an iOS app for running and tracking Magic: The Gathering budget leagues. This policy describes how the app handles your data.

---

## Data collection

League Keeper **does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data** on servers operated by us or third parties. The app does not require an account or sign-in.

---

## Data storage

All data you create in the app (players, tournaments, pods, standings, achievements, settings) is stored **only on your device** using Apple’s standard on-device storage (SwiftData). No league data is sent to the internet.

---

## Third parties

The app does not integrate analytics, advertising, or other third-party SDKs that collect or transmit your data.

---

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please use the support contact listed in the app or on the App Store support page (e.g. GitHub Issues or the email/support URL you provide).

---

## Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will reflect the latest version. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
